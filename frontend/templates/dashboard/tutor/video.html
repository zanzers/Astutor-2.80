Video requirements

Fufill the requirements to get aprroved

Recommended

  • Keep the video between 30 seconds to 2 minutes max.
  • Record in a Portrait mode at eye level.
  • Use good lighting with a neutral background.
  • Place the camera on a stable surface to avoid shakiness.
  • Ensure your face and eyes are clearly visible (unless covered for religious reasons).
  • Talk about your teaching experience and any relevant certifications.
  • Greet your students warmly and encourage them to book a lesson.

Avoid

  • Your surname or any contact details.
  • Logos, links, or branding.
  • Slideshows or presentations.
  • Other people in the video frame.
